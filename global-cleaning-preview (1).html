Do you service all areas of Sydney?
Yes — we cover the full Greater Sydney metro area including the CBD, inner suburbs, western Sydney, the Hills District, and Macarthur. Call us to confirm your area.

No. We work on a flexible month-to-month basis. We believe in earning your business every single month, not trapping you in a contract.

Absolutely. All team members are police-checked, hold relevant licences, and we carry $20M public liability insurance. Full compliance documentation available on request.
Can you clean outside business hours?
Yes — we specialise in after-hours, early morning, and weekend cleaning so your business is never disrupted during trading hours.

Our Story

We Built the Company We Wished Existed

Back in 2009, our founders were frustrated. Business owners they knew kept telling the same story — cleaners who didn't show up, cut corners, or vanished after a month. The commercial cleaning industry was full of promises and short on follow-through.

So they started Global Cleaning Group with a simple premise: do what you say you'll do, care about the outcome, and treat every client's workplace like it's your own.

Over 15 years later, that philosophy has built a company with 500+ loyal clients across Greater Sydney — from family-owned SMEs to national logistics companies — and a team that's genuinely proud of the work they deliver every day.

We're still Sydney-owned, still personally invested, and still picking up the phone when you call.
