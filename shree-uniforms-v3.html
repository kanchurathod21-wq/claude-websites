Our Foundation

Built on Decades of
Trusted Craftsmanship

Shree Enterprise Uniforms was founded in Mumbai in 1993 with a simple but powerful belief: every business deserves uniforms that are as professional as they are. Starting from a small workshop in Sewri, we grew from supplying a handful of local restaurants to becoming one of Mumbai's most respected uniform manufacturers.

For three decades, we have watched our clients succeed — their staff walking proudly in our uniforms across some of Mumbai's most iconic establishments. Domino's, Café Coffee Day, Rebel Foods, Quess Corp — the trust these household names have placed in us is our greatest credential.

Today, we operate a fully in-house manufacturing facility with a capacity of 1,000 pieces per day, 365 days a year. Every step — design, cutting, stitching, embroidery, printing and packaging — happens under one roof in Sewri, Mumbai. This gives us complete control over quality and delivery timelines.
