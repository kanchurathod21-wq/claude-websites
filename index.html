Yes! We are a registered CCS provider. Most families receive significant government support through the Child Care Subsidy, which can reduce your out-of-pocket costs considerably. We can help you understand what you're entitled to.
We are open Monday to Friday, 7:00am to 6:00pm. We are closed on public holidays and for a short break over Christmas and New Year. We recommend calling ahead to discuss which days work best for your family.
A spare set of clothes, sunscreen (we also supply), a hat, and any comfort items for younger children. All meals and snacks are provided. We supply nappies, wipes, and all learning materials.

Demand is high and spots fill quickly. We recommend getting on our waitlist as early as possible — even during pregnancy. Book a tour first so you can meet the team and see if we're the right fit for your family.
We take allergies extremely seriously. All staff are first-aid trained and allergy-aware. We create individual dietary plans for every child and our kitchen is briefed daily. We are a nut-free centre.
